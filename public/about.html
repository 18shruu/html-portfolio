About Me

Over time, I found myself gravitating more toward the software side, leading me to pursue personal projects, online courses, and hands-on development. These experiences helped me build a strong understanding of Python, Java, and modern web frameworks like Django and Spring Boot.

I've also gained valuable experience collaborating with cross-functional teams, debugging critical issues, and writing clean, scalable code. I always strive to write maintainable solutions and continuously improve my technical and communication skills.

Outside of coding, I’m passionate about learning emerging technologies, especially in the areas of artificial intelligence and cloud computing. I enjoy participating in technical discussions, attending developer meetups, and mentoring peers whenever I get the chance. I’m also involved in community service and have volunteered to teach underprivileged children, combining my love for education and social impact.

I believe in continuous learning, taking initiative, and building solutions that make a difference. Whether it’s a new project, a collaborative opportunity, or simply a knowledge-sharing session, I’m always excited to connect and grow.
